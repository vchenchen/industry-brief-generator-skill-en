# Launch Copy

## LinkedIn

I built a universal industry brief generator for Codex.

Input an industry, target market, focus topics, exclusions, and final use. The skill first generates 20 sourced candidate topics. After the user selects 8, it creates the final brief, poster, and channel-ready copy.

The key idea is candidate-first publishing. The skill does not decide the final stories for you. It gives you a curated pool, keeps source links visible, and waits for human selection.

Trust rules matter:

- no fabricated sources
- no fake dates
- no soft copy repackaged as industry news
- no generic finance filler

Useful for consultants, market research teams, international business teams, content operators, industry media, and sales/BD teams.

GitHub: https://github.com/vchenchen/industry-brief-generator-skill-english
